Whether in a Starbucks coffee shop, an overnight youth hostel, or a compartment of train, I like to start a conversation with the person who gives me a good first impression. This is how I explore world’s cultures and ideologies.

As I grow up, I extend this habit into more professional forms—public relations. During the undergraduate years, I engaged actively as a strategic maker and public liaison with projects and social activities. In 2009, I joined AIESEC, an international student-run organization for global internship exchange and potential development. Apart from marketing, business negotiations with local companies for the Incoming Exchange Function, I also launched an international project, Green Power Project in Sichuan Province, China. The latter one gained me precious experiences in management, strategic planning, coordination, promotion, and external relations.
