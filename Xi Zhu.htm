Public Relations Project--DancedIn Campaign

Start from November, 2013

As a dance fan, have you ever thought about an arresting dance identity on the internet?

Have you ever imagined the effects of promoting your dance work through social media?

Do you want more people know your creativity and passion for dance? Are you looking for dancers with similar styles or an optimal dance community?


DancedIn is an online public relations campaign aiming to present each participator’s unique dance identity and realize virtual-to-actual dance interactions. The vision is to let dance fans express themselves and benefit each other by sharing and communications.

For more information, please go to: DancedIn Homgpage
